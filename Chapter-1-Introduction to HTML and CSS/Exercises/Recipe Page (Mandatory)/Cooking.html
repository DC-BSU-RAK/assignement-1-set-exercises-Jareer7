New York Cheesecake

This classic New York cheesecake is ultra rich, dense, and creamy with a slightly tangy flavor and a smooth finish. Perfect for any special occasion or just because you deserve it.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 4 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press firmly into the bottom of the pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and fluffy.
  4. Add sugar and vanilla, and beat until combined.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, beating on low speed after each addition.
  6. Mix in sour cream and heavy cream until fully incorporated.
  7. Pour batter over the crust and smooth the top.
  8. Bake for 1 hour. Turn off oven and let cheesecake sit inside for 1 more hour with the door slightly open.
  9. Remove from oven and chill in the fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  10. Slice, serve, and enjoy!
