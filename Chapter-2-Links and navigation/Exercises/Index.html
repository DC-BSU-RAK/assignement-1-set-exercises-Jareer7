Stranger Things

Overview

*Stranger Things* is a sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s. It follows a group of kids in the town of Hawkins as they encounter supernatural forces, secret government experiments, and an alternate dimension known as the Upside Down.

Key Information

  • Release Year: 2016
  • Created By: The Duffer Brothers
  • Genre: Sci-Fi, Horror, Drama
  • Seasons: 4 (5th upcoming)

Cast & Characters

  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
